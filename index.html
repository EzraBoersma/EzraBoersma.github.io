Our Skills and Services

We provide certified professional service for your work needs.

Wellsite Management

Trained, insured and certified, with over 10 years of patch experience, We have the professional consultants you're looking for. We can help with upgrades, clean-ups, set-ups, and virtually any other operation needs your site requires.

Call-in Field Support

Need a job done - quickly? Call a professional site alarm respondent to address your site needs 24/7 in case of any alarm-code failures. From our remote base, we can be on-site in a matter of just two hours or less, depending on the location.

Consulting Services

We are accepting short and long term contracts for consultants. We specialize in optimizing production, process engineering, offering technical observations and experience-based solutions.

Northern Roots

Welcome to Red Earth Creek, Alberta. We're in our element - and we can help you.

Any good project starts with a solid foundation, and we want to share our experience with the industry. Based in the wilderness of Northern Canada, Boersma Oilfields Services brings you local expertise and professionalism, that you can rely on.

As second generation operators from a small community, we understand the importance of doing a good job for our employer, and for our community. We have experience with jobs big, and small. From rig set-up, to daily site management, to equipment transportation - keep us as your reliable on-call contact.
